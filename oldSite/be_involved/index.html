Contribute:

If you or your agency or company are in a position to do so, TIPS On Art would welcome your support in the form of financial underwriting for our programs and services. We are able to provide all of our services at no cost and make them available to the entire community because of the generous spirit of our Core leaders who donate their expertise as well. To maintain and advance our standard of excellence and complete accessibility for all, we need your support. Do what you can and send a check made payable to "TIPS On Art" to: Donna Wetegrove, 6019 Abilene Trail, Austin, Texas 78749. Thank you in advance!

TIPS On Art is solely supported by individual, foundation, corporate grants and donations and exhibit sales as we grow into membership dues, special events, online product sales and eventually facility rental fees.

Donate Online:

When you click the "Donate" button, you will be transferred to a secure site that is supported by PayPal, our authorized payment processor. A space will be provided for you to enter the amount you wish to donate. If you already have a PayPal account, then you will only need to login. Otherwise, you will need to provide PayPal with your contact information (name, address and email) and your credit card information. The information that you enter will be secure and private and PayPal will verify the information. You will also be asked to Provide a password for your account so that you can login in the future without having to enter your information again.

Note: For every transaction, PayPal receives 2.9% (of total donation) + .30 for the service that they provide and TIPS On Art receives the rest.
