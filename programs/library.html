Learning Library Segments

� 'tips' are those little gems of information shared by others that we all cherish along our individual journeys. Enjoy the journey!

Our Learning Library Segments invite the public into lively discussions with artisans and arts organizations in a wide range of mediums and performance styles.

We add new interviews each year so be sure to check back with us regularly. Each segment represents the artist or director's personal views and by no means is intended to suggest their methods are the only way to interact with each medium. In fact, each artist encourages experimentation to find your unique style. Enjoy, learn and be inspired! Then, let us know what you think and what you want to know more about.

We commit a strong focus on Austin- area high schools and libraries as a means of supplementing the educational tools available to instructors.

Bringing creative information into the learning environment to inspire our next generation of artists is part of our mission ... linking our artistic opportunities to strengthen our community and our world. Welcome to TIPS On Art � Learning Segments On The Web!
