Film

In Development - Movies have taken the place of the novel in American cultural relevance and discourse. Film, both popular and explosive on the screen and at the box office, and more contemplative art-house statements, is the art form of the 21st Century. Other than in the back lots of Hollywood and the streets of New York, there are few places in the nation that can compete with the prolificacy and eclecticism of Austin's film culture. TIPS On Art will further Austin film by:

  • Offering listings and showings
  • Offering listings of casting calls
  • Providing valuable networking opportunities for filmmakers
  • Co-sponsoring filmmakers' workshops and speakers' forums
  • Showing selected films at special venues
  • Offering film clips online
  • Co-sponsoring an annual film event and contest

Sharing tips. Advancing art. Expanding minds.
We are better together.
