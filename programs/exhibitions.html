Exhibitions

The collaborative nature in which this art comes to you through these local business and arts organizations is similar to the spirit that is behind one of Austin's most up and coming arts based learning organizations. The original art presentations are a collaborative community effort to enhance our built environments and our souls. Each exhibit provides varied target audiences with information on the artist's background, artistic inspiration and/or technique used for the work being viewed.
