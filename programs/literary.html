Literary Series

In Development - Literature can be the most difficult, sublime and resonant of the arts. It can also be the most flat out fun. Just ask all those kids in line to buy the latest Harry Potter or Lemony Snicket book. One of the blessings of the medium is its accessibility and portability. Books take up little space and can be taken anywhere. Whether it is plotted, classic story-telling or avant, mind-bending poetry, TIPS On Art will endeavor to bring Austin writers and readers the best literary resources and platforms possible by:

  • Offering listings of readings and literary events
  • Offering listings of published works by Texas authors
  • Providing valuable networking opportunities for writers and publishers
  • Co-sponsoring writers/publishers workshops and forums
  • Co-sponsoring poetry and story readings at special venues
  • Offering audio readings online
  • Publishing an annual literary publication, Achtung!, which will focus on the works of Texas writers and themes
  • Holding annual short story, poetry and essay contests
  • Creating a space for interactive writing projects online for all ages and levels of experience
  • Working in cooperation with Austin's libraries to help bring all their amazing resources to the forefront
We believe everyone has a story to tell and intend to create Austin's own version of the much loved Selected Shorts program from Symphony Space.

Sharing tips. Advancing art. Expanding minds.
We are better together.
