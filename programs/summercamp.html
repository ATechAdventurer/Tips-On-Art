"I AM CREATIVE" Summer Camp
for ART::MIND::COMMUNITY

Tips on Art is excited to be sponsoring a summer camp for kids, ages 8-14. This camp will introduce your children to a variety of art mediums.

Activities will include collage, drawing, sculpting, painting, photography, swimming, music and stories- with a focus on applying Sustainable Practices and Recycled Material.

Each week, the outcome of a group art project will be dontated to a local assisted living center or homeless support services center to brighten the environment of these important community centers.

Kids can work towards Girl Scout badge work for Art to Wear,Boy ScoutArt Activity Pins, and Campfire Creative Emblems!

All campers will receive a souvenir t-shirt.

Sharing tips. Advancing art. Expanding minds.
We are better together.
